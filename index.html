Our History

Like any other original place, we have our own, special history. The idea for the restaurant came unexpectedly to the founders. While walking through the woods, the creator of our restaurant got stuck hundreds of kilometers from the nearest settlement. Far from civilization and communication, they had to equip themselves a simple life at the right time, get and cook their own food.
